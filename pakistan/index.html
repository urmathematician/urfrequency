Pakistan's Space Program (1966-2025)

Pakistan's space efforts began in 1961 with SUPARCO. While it never developed independent orbital launch capability, it was a pioneer in sounding rockets among developing nations.

1. Rocket Launches Overview

Category Details
Sounding Rockets (1961-1972) 200+ Rehbar series launches (with NASA/French assistance)
Orbital Launches 0 (all satellites launched by foreign rockets)
First Satellite Badr-1 (1990, launched by China)
Notable Satellites PRSS-1 (remote sensing), PAKSAT-MM1 (communications)

2. Key Milestones

Year Event

1990 Badr-1 satellite (China-launched)
2011 Paksat-1R communications satellite
2018 PRSS-1 remote sensing satellite
2024 Contributed to China's Chang'e-6 lunar mission
